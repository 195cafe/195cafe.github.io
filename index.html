Our Story

A place to slow
down & savour

Tucked away at number 195, our café was born from a simple belief — that a truly great cup of coffee, paired with something baked fresh that morning, can make any day feel a little more complete.

We source our beans with care, train our baristas to treat every brew as a small craft, and bake our toast fresh each day — with recipes that feel like a warm embrace.
